The Sunken Court

Beneath the Glass Marsh, the Sunken Court keeps the old bargains. Its heralds speak only in tide-signs, and its writ runs as far as the brackish water reaches.

Rank and Office

Three offices hold the Court together across the drowned seasons.

Office Duty
Tidewarden Reads the marsh and calls the floods
Reed Herald Carries the Court's writ to the fen villages
